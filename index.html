The Architecture

Four Layers of Legal Intelligence

A precision-engineered hybrid AI stack that combines the depth of statistical machine learning with the reasoning power of modern large language models — all running entirely on-premise.

01 / INTAKE ENGINE
⚖
Adaptive Discovery
Acts as a Senior Counsel — not a chatbot. If your case description is vague, the system uses an embedded LLM to ask exactly what's missing. It detects issue type (Section 269T, 40A(3), etc.) and switches logic accordingly.
Ollama / phi3:mini Contextual NLU Streamlit UI
02 / INTELLIGENCE CORE
◈
Hybrid ML Engine
TF-IDF + Logistic Regression for fast legal issue tagging. MiniLM-L6 sentence embeddings to match your fact pattern by semantic similarity. Random Forest outcome predictor trained on 12,000 real ITAT verdicts.
MiniLM-L6 Random Forest TF-IDF BERTopic
03 / RETRIEVAL SYSTEM
◉
Vector Search at Scale
FAISS-powered vector database enables lightning-fast similarity search across 12,000+ judgments. Retrieves the exact "Key Ratio" — the judicial logic used in comparable cases — in milliseconds. Drift-resistance ensures recent precedents carry greater weight.
FAISS UMAP HDBSCAN Decay Weights
04 / STRATEGY SYNTHESIS
▣
Grounded Brief Generator
Feeds retrieved legal data and identified facts into a local LLM with a high-precision prompt. Outputs a complete defense strategy: precedent citations, adversary attack points, merit win % vs. technical relief %, and a downloadable PDF brief.
llama3 / phi3 RAG Pipeline PDF Export

Investor Q&A

Questions We Expect

Q
"Why won't OpenAI or a Big-4 firm just build this?"
▶
OpenAI hallucinates on local Indian statutes and has no access to our cleaned, structured ITAT dataset. Big-4 firms have the data but not this specific hybrid architecture combining statistical ML with LLM reasoning. We sit at the intersection of both — a moat that requires years of proprietary data collection and specialized model training to replicate.
Q
"What happens when the law changes?"
▶
Our Drift Resistance system includes a decay_stale_weights function that automatically reduces the influence of older precedents relative to recent judgments. As new ITAT decisions are added to the dataset, the system re-weights the retrieval model. The Expert Feedback Loop further accelerates this: when a CA flags an outdated strategy, that signal propagates through the training pipeline.
Q
"What is the liability model? Can AI give legal advice?"
▶
LIOS is a research and strategy assistance tool — a co-pilot, not a replacement for professional judgment. All outputs are explicitly labelled as AI-generated research for professional review. The licensed CA or Advocate retains all professional responsibility. This is the same model used by legal research platforms like Manupatra and SCC Online, which are widely adopted across India.
Q
"How do you make money and what does unit economics look like?"
▶
Tiered SaaS: Lite at ₹2,999/month for individuals; Enterprise at custom pricing for firms. A single Enterprise client saving 500 hours/year at ₹3,000/hour billing rates = ₹15 Lakh in recovered capacity annually — making our product essentially free from an ROI standpoint. At 1% penetration of the CA/lawyer market on Lite pricing alone, ARR exceeds ₹540 Crore.
Q
"Can you expand beyond ITAT to High Courts and Supreme Court?"
▶
Yes — that is Phase 2 of our roadmap. The architecture is modality-agnostic; we can ingest and vectorize any structured judgment corpus. High Court and Supreme Court tax judgments will expand the dataset to 50,000+ decisions and extend our reach to senior advocates arguing constitutional tax matters.
